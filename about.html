This Luxury Boutique is commited to brining you the best (and most luxurious!) fashion experience possible. We value customers' love of the finer things, and aim to deliver that with every item we make. That refined approach is central to what we do, and ensures that when you put together a stunning outfit, you know it was made with the care and precision you deserve.



1950

Luxuria Boutique was founded October, 1950, by four fashionistas intent on creating the next big hit to the fashion market.

1953

For many years, the team remained small, and so did the brand. The company was struggling to get off the ground and into the public eye.

2017

1955

By a stroke of luck, Luxuria Boutique garnered a brand deal with a celebrity, bringing a boom of business that felt like the lucky break everyone was waiting for.

1980

Luxuria Boutique continued to grow steadily through the 60s, 70s, and into the 80s, until a tragic fire set the company back by thousands of sales.

1999

It wasn't until 1999 that the company was truly back on its feet, competing with the new age of interconnectivity and advertisement that came with the fast expanding 'world wide web'.

2004

Luxuria Boutique took a gamble; investing a large chunk of company resources into ecommerce with most of their sales happening offline at the time, but it paid off very well, and Luxuria Boutique became bigger than ever.

2019

And that's where the company is now, constantly evolving along with the internet age and the fashion tastes of its customers, like you!
